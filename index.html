About the Band

Hailing from Falun, Sweden, Sabaton has been bringing the many epics of history to the center stage since 1999! Sabaton leaves no story sunsung, as tales of heroism are spun for all to see, as history’s most noble men and women are given the attention they deserve. Tales of rising evils, burning hopes, valiant defenders, and shining examples of human courage are all within Sabaton’s domain, delivered to you one album at a time.
